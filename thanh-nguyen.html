Personal Information

My full name is Thanh Nguyen Trong or most of my friends know it in Vietnamese as Nguyễn Trọng Thành. I’m currently a first-year, first-semester student enrolling at RMIT University Vietnam, majoring in Information Technology and my student ID is s3818993. About my background, I spent my formative years in Hanoi, Vietnam but came to Ho Chi Minh City about three months ago to study and also to start a new life. During the high school years, I used to pursue graphic design professionally, participating in extracurricular activities, projects, events and so on. Because of that, when applying to universities, my friends thought I would choose a design-related major. But due to a change of heart at the last minute, combines with the support of my brother, who is a National University of Singapore (NUS) alumnus majoring in Computer Science, I switched to Information Technology. But still, despite all of that, I still maintain my love for graphic design and arts in general. I enjoy drawing and doing creative design in my leisure time. It is a good change of air once in a while to help with the constant pressure from work at school. And of course, because of my background in design, my interest in IT would lies at web design, UI/UX design, front-end web development, and to ensure a seamless experience for the user. Additionally, I find the information security sector to pique my interest. Moreover, I have little to no experience in IT other than some basics in Python alongside with HTML & CSS.
